## CONFIDENTIALITY OBLIGATIONS

### 1. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

Confidential Information includes but is not limited to:

- Individual business financial data including EBITDA, sales figures, and targets
- Business processes, workflows, and operational procedures
- Customer data, supplier information, and contact details
- Strategic plans, business models, and competitive information
- Individual business identities, ownership details, and performance metrics
- Any information marked as "Confidential" or that would reasonably be considered confidential

### 2. MUTUAL OBLIGATIONS

Both parties agree to:

- **Maintain strict confidentiality** of all disclosed information
- **Use information solely** for the purpose of Business Scorecard development and analysis
- **Limit access** to employees and contractors with legitimate need-to-know
- **Implement reasonable security measures** to protect confidential information
- **Return or destroy** all confidential materials upon request or project completion
- **Never disclose individual business data** to other participating businesses without explicit consent

### 3. PERMITTED DISCLOSURES

Information may be disclosed if:

- Required by law or court order (with prior notice where possible)
- Already in the public domain through no breach of this agreement
- Independently developed without use of confidential information
- Previously known to the receiving party without confidentiality restrictions
- Aggregated in a manner that does not identify individual businesses

## DATA PROTECTION & SECURITY

### 4. GDPR COMPLIANCE

BrandedAI commits to:

- **Process personal data** only as necessary for scorecard development and analysis
- **Implement appropriate technical and organisational measures** for data security
- **Provide data processing agreement** as required under GDPR Article 28
- **Notify client immediately** of any data breaches or security incidents
- **Delete or return personal data** upon project completion or client request

### 5. SECURITY MEASURES

Minimum security standards:

- Encryption in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols
- Access controls with unique user credentials and multi-factor authentication
- Regular security updates and vulnerability assessments
- Secure development practices including code review and testing
- Incident response procedures for security breaches

### 6. DATA SEGREGATION

BrandedAI specifically agrees to:

- **Maintain strict segregation** of individual business data within the platform
- **Ensure participating businesses** can only view their own individual data
- **Provide aggregated summaries only** in a format that protects individual identities
- **Never share raw data** with unauthorised parties including other participating businesses

### 7. MINIMAL ACCESS PRINCIPLE

BrandedAI commits to the following access restrictions:

- **No routine access:** BrandedAI will not routinely view, browse, or access individual business financial data
- **Support access only:** Individual data will only be accessed when strictly necessary for technical support, debugging, or at the explicit request of the data owner
- **Self-service tools:** BrandedAI will provide self-service data upload functionality enabling Chester Brethren representatives to import historical data without BrandedAI handling raw files
- **Audit trail:** All administrative access to individual business data will be logged with timestamp, user, and purpose
- **Aggregation preference:** Where possible, BrandedAI will work with aggregated or anonymised data for development, testing, and analysis purposes
